Snapshot Explained
This snapshot shows where you stand across major AI platforms. It's a topline read, not a full AI Visibility Audit — it provides directional signals, not depth analysis or recommendations.
Method: AI responses collected via an automated procedure across major AI models over a span of days. Prompts include typical customer queries during the exploratory and consideration stages, lightly customized for the brand category.
How percentages are calculated: Mention rate reflects the share of AI responses in which the brand appeared. Citation share reflects the brand's portion of all source links cited across the full prompt set.

The new GEO playbook
AI doesn't see marketing silos — it looks across all of them to assemble and generate answers, and that can change by many factors: AI model algorithms and type of question.

AI Visibility relies upon an integrated strategy with coordinated channel plans that leverage the role of each channel.

Health of the Whole Owned: The truth anchor — your site, product pages, and content are a definitive foundation AI can draw from. Earned: Signals of trust — press, reviews, and third-party coverage indicate to AI models that your brand is credible. Shared: Community proof — forums, social, and user content show AI that real people are talking about you. Paid: The amplifier — drives awareness and branded search volume that feeds the entire ecosystem.
