Experience Columbus — Entertainment

Arena District

The heart of Columbus nightlife and entertainment — professional sports, live music, comedy clubs, and dozens of restaurants and bars.

Request Information
BUX: Panel

Where Columbus Comes Alive.

The Arena District is Columbus's premier entertainment destination — home to Nationwide Arena (NHL Blue Jackets), Huntington Park (minor league baseball), dozens of restaurants and bars, comedy clubs, and live music venues. It's the go-to spot for post-event socializing, team outings, and group entertainment. Walking distance to the Greater Columbus Convention Center makes it ideal for conference attendees looking for nightlife and fun.

Group Fun

Group Entertainment & Outings

The Arena District is perfect for group outings — Blue Jackets games, comedy shows, group dining at top restaurants, and bar crawls. Let us coordinate the logistics so your attendees can enjoy the experience.
