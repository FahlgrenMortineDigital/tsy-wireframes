Our Team

Ohio State Events serves as your central point of access to the extraordinary depth of talent across The Ohio State University. From venue sales and event planning to catering, technology, and on-site operations, we connect you with experienced professionals throughout campus to bring your event to life.

Our partners represent rentable spaces and venues across Athletics, Student Life, academic facilities, and other signature campus locations. Together, we operate as one coordinated team aligning expertise, services, and resources to ensure your experience is seamless, strategic, and distinctly Buckeye.
