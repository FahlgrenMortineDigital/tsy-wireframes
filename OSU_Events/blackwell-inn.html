Where Buckeye Hospitality Meets Boutique Elegance.

The Blackwell Inn & Pfahl Conference Center is Ohio State's full-service boutique hotel, owned and operated by the university and located directly across from Ohio Stadium. With 151 beautifully appointed guest rooms, personalized service, and a dedicated events team, The Blackwell offers event planners a seamless lodging experience that keeps attendees comfortable and connected to campus.

What sets The Blackwell apart is its unique combination of hotel accommodations and the Pfahl Conference Center under one roof. With over 18,000 square feet of flexible meeting and event space, your attendees can sleep, meet, dine, and network without ever leaving the building — making it the ideal home base for multi-day conferences, executive retreats, and campus events.

Location

In the Heart of Campus

The Blackwell sits at the center of Ohio State's campus, within walking distance of Ohio Stadium, the Wexner Center for the Arts, The Oval, and the vibrant Short North Arts District. The university's free CABS campus bus system connects guests to every corner of campus, making it easy to explore everything Ohio State and Columbus have to offer.
