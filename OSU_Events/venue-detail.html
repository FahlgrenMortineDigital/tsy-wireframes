Where Great Events Come Together.

The Fawcett Center is one of Ohio State's premier event and conference venues, offering flexible indoor and outdoor spaces for groups of 10 to 500. Located on the Olentangy River Road corridor near campus, it features a grand ballroom, auditorium, multiple breakout rooms, an outdoor terrace, and a full-service kitchen.

Whether hosting a multi-day conference, wedding reception, corporate retreat, or awards ceremony, the Fawcett Center's dedicated staff handles every detail — from room setup and catering to audiovisual support and event coordination — so you can focus on what matters most: your guests.

Dining & Catering

On-Site Catering & Dining Options

The Fawcett Center offers full-service, in-house catering for all events. From plated dinners and buffets to coffee breaks and cocktail receptions, our culinary team handles menus of every scale and style.

Preferred caterers: In-house catering is required for most events. Contact us for approved external caterer options.
