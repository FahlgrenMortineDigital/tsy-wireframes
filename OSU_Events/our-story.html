Your Gateway to Ohio State

The Ohio State Office of Events and Experiences is your gateway to the legendary spaces and immersive experiences of The Ohio State University. As the university's central resource for hosting campus events, we connect you to the people, places, and services that bring your vision to life seamlessly and thoughtfully.

Whether you're planning a conference, symposium, competition, retreat, or celebration, Ohio State Events works across the university to serve as your connector — linking you with venues, vendors, and campus partners to ensure every detail enhances the guest experience.

Faculty and Staff

Partner with us to:

  • Bring new conferences or signature events to campus
  • Develop and submit event bid proposals
  • Host or coordinate site visits
  • Plan complex events across multiple venues or campus partners
  • Get strategic guidance on venue selection and event flow
  • Infuse your event with Buckeye spirit and Columbus connections

External Event Planners

Partner with us to:

  • Explore campus venues for conferences, competitions, meetings, and celebrations
  • Navigate the site selection and proposal process
  • Coordinate site visits and venue tours
  • Connect with trusted campus partners for catering, technology, and event services
  • Create memorable campus experiences for your attendees

How We Work

A Unified Event Experience

We help coordinate spaces, resources, and partners to ensure a seamless experience for you and your guests.

Bid Support

Competitive Bid Support

We partner with you throughout the bid and site selection process to bring your event to campus.

Venues

Venue Matching

With access to more than 150 rentable spaces, we guide you to the right location — or combination of locations — for your event.

Full Service

Full-Service Event Support

We connect you with experienced professionals across dining, catering, technology, and event planning. One coordinated process. One streamlined contract and invoice.

Experiences

Buckeye & Columbus Connections

From stadium tours to merchandise and curated experiences throughout Columbus, we help you create a true campus-and-city experience.
