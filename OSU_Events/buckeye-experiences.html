Buckeye Experiences

BUX: Panel

Experiences Only Ohio State Can Offer.

Ohio State is more than a venue — it's a destination. Ohio State Events offers curated, one-of-a-kind add-ons that transform ordinary events into unforgettable ones. From walking the tunnel at Ohio Stadium to hosting a private reception at the Jack Nicklaus Museum, these are the moments that set your event apart and give attendees something truly unique to take home.

Whether it's attending a Buckeye game day with VIP hospitality, exploring the Schottenstein Center behind the scenes, or gathering your group for an exclusive campus tour, these signature experiences create lasting memories. They're the stories attendees tell for years — and the reason they'll want to come back.
