Thank You for Your Request!

We've received your event inquiry and a member of the Ohio State Events team will be in touch within 1–2 business days with tailored recommendations.

What Happens Next
1. Confirmation email sent — Check your inbox for a copy of your submission and a reference number.
2. Team review — Our event coordinators will review your needs, preferred dates, and service requests.
3. Personalized response — We'll reach out with venue options, service packages, and a customized proposal for your event.
