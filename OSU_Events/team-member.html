Biography

A longer biographical narrative about this team member's career, their journey to Ohio State, and what drives their work. This section provides depth and personality beyond the summary — covering their background, career highlights, and connection to the university.

This person leads the Ohio State Events team, overseeing all aspects of university events, hospitality, and campus experience programming. With over 15 years of experience in higher education event management, they bring a strategic vision and hands-on approach to making Ohio State the premier destination for conferences, meetings, and celebrations. They joined Ohio State in 2015 after a distinguished career in hospitality management at several major universities and convention centers.

Education

  • M.S., Hospitality Management — Cornell University
  • B.A., Communication — The Ohio State University

Areas of Expertise

  • Large-scale conference and event planning
  • University hospitality and guest services
  • Venue management and campus space optimization
  • Strategic partnerships and vendor relations
  • Budget oversight and resource allocation
  • Team leadership and professional development

Professional Affiliations

  • International Association of Venue Managers (IAVM)
  • Meeting Professionals International (MPI)
  • Association of Collegiate Conference and Events Directors-International (ACCED-I)
  • Columbus Hospitality Council — Board Member
